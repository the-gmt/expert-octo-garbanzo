Common questions

Canada has two major credit bureaus — Equifax and TransUnion. Lenders don't always report to the same one, and some report to both at different times. If a fraudster tries to open credit in your name, that activity might only show up at one bureau. Monitoring just one means you could miss the early warning signs entirely. Platinum and Ultimate watch both, every day.
